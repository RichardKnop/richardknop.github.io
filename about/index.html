About me

Experienced distributed systems engineer, architect, open source contributor, Gopher and Pythonista. A generalist experienced in designing scalable platforms and applications and writing clean well-tested code.

Having designed and developed greenfield API platforms for several startups that allowed them to quickly get to market and become leaders in their fields, I have gained valuable technical skills and significant experience.

I focus on using modern open source technology and latest dev ops industry trends. Currently I use Go as my main programming language and design small decoupled components which I deploy as Docker containers on CoreOS hosts either on IaaS cloud provider (AWS, GC or Azure) or bare metal.

This approach is referred to as microservices and offers several benefits to organisations. I prefer having a fully automated and easily reproducible environments. I use Terraform and Ansible to achieve this goal.

Other than that, I am very keen on testing, continuous integration and best practices and I am a frequent contributor to open source software.
