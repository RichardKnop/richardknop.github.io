About me

I am a generalist experienced in creation of scalable platforms and applications. I have been working on micro services, SOAs, mobile and web apps, video transcoding.

I prefer composition over inheritance, SOA over monolithic systems (micro services). I like to remove inefficiencies, automate manual tasks and to think of ways to simplify complex processes.

My favourite language is Python followed by Go and JavaScript. For server configuration I like to use Puppet or Ansible, RabbitMQ is message queue of choice.

I used other languages as well (Objective C, PHP), many frameworks (e.g. Django, Backbone) and both relational and NoSQL databases (Postgres, MySQL, Elasticsearch, DynamoDB, Cassandra, Redis).
